Our Mission

From doubles to pelau, we bring authentic Trini flavors to every plate. Our goal is simple: serve warmth, laughter, and comfort through food that tells a story.

What started as a small family kitchen now welcomes guests from across Chicago who come for the taste of home, and stay for the sense of belonging.
