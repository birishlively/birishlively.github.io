Our Mission

From doubles to pelau, we strive to bring authentic Trini flavors to every plate. Our mission goes beyond just serving food, it's about sharing the heart and soul of Trinidad and Tobago with every meal we make. Each dish represents a story, a memory, and a piece of the island that we're proud to share with our community.

We are committed to sourcing fresh, local ingredients and supporting sustainable practices to ensure that every bite is not only delicious but also responsible. At MAKO, we believe in creating a welcoming space where everyone feels like family, and every meal is a celebration of culture, community, and connection.

Join us in our journey to bring a taste of Trinidad to Chicago, one dish at a time.
